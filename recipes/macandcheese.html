Mac and Cheese


DESCRIPTION

Mac and Cheese, a beloved classic comfort dish, is the epitome of creamy indulgence and irresistible cheesiness. Originating from the United States, this dish has earned a permanent place in the hearts and palates of food enthusiasts worldwide. Imagine tender elbow macaroni enveloped in a velvety cheese sauce that boasts the perfect blend of sharpness and creaminess. Mac and Cheese is a timeless, crowd-pleasing favorite that transcends age and culinary preferences, making it the ultimate go-to dish for a satisfying and comforting meal.

INGREDIENTS

  1. 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  2. 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  3. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  6. 1/4 teaspoon mustard powder
  7. 2 1/2 cups whole milk
  8. 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  9. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  10. 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (optional for topping)

STEPS

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour, salt, black pepper, and mustard powder to form a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring no lumps form. Continue to cook and whisk until the mixture thickens.
  4. Reduce the heat and add the shredded cheddar and Parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese is completely melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce, ensuring every piece is coated evenly.
  6. If desired, transfer the mac and cheese to a baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and bake until golden and bubbly.
  7. Serve the mac and cheese hot, reveling in its comforting goodness and cheesy perfection.
